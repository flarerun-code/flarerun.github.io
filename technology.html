Our Focus
Building quantitative infrastructure for systematic participation in financial markets.

Arcnine Research focuses on designing and operating data-driven trading systems where research, execution, and risk management are integrated into a single quantitative framework. Our infrastructure supports systematic strategies across derivatives and index markets while maintaining strict operational discipline and capital controls.

Philosophy
Markets reward structured research and disciplined systems.

Systematic Research

Market behaviour is analysed using statistical models, historical datasets, and microstructure research. Strategies are developed only when supported by measurable and repeatable evidence.

Engineering Discipline

Trading infrastructure is engineered with reliability and determinism in mind, ensuring that research results can transition consistently from simulation to live trading environments.

Integrated Risk Controls

Risk management is embedded directly within the trading system through position limits, automated safeguards, and continuous monitoring of capital exposure.

Capabilities
Technology and research infrastructure designed for systematic trading operations.

Quantitative Research

Strategy development involves rigorous hypothesis testing, large-scale backtesting, and statistical validation to ensure robustness across market conditions.

Automated Execution Systems

Proprietary execution infrastructure enables systematic order placement, latency-aware trade management, and consistent interaction with exchange markets.

Monitoring & Post-Trade Analysis

Live systems are continuously monitored with comprehensive logging and post-trade analytics to evaluate strategy behaviour and operational performance.
