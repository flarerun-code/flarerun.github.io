About VectorPoint Capital

VectorPoint Capital is a quantitative trading firm built on systematic research, precision engineering, and disciplined risk management.

Our work centers on identifying durable market structures through data, mathematics, and probabilistic reasoning. We emphasize robustness over prediction and process over discretion.

By integrating technology, research, and control into a unified framework, we aim to create strategies that remain resilient across cycles, regimes, and evolving market conditions.
