About VectorPoint Capital

VectorPoint Capital is a quantitative proprietary trading firm focused on systematic strategies across global financial markets. We design and deploy algorithmic trading systems grounded in data, mathematics, and disciplined risk management. Our approach emphasizes empirical research, robust infrastructure, and continuous adaptation to evolving market dynamics.
