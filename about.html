About Us

VectorPoint Capital is a quantitative proprietary trading firm focused on systematic strategies across global financial markets. We specialize in building algorithmic trading systems driven by data, mathematics, and disciplined risk management.

Our liquidity provision contributes to the efficiency and resilience of modern financial markets, particularly during periods of elevated volatility. Every strategy we deploy is designed with robustness, scalability, and capital efficiency as core objectives.

Our Process

Think

We identify and analyze market behavior to uncover structural inefficiencies. Ideas are rigorously debated, stress-tested, and validated through quantitative research.

Build

We engineer highly optimized algorithmic trading systems using resilient infrastructure, automation, and low-latency execution frameworks.

Strategize

Our strategies are constructed using advanced mathematical and statistical modeling, ensuring decisions are grounded in probability and empirical evidence.

Trade

We trade systematically across a broad range of asset classes on algorithmic markets worldwide, maintaining consistency across market regimes.

Risk & Control

Risk management is embedded at every layer of our trading lifecycle, with real-time monitoring, exposure limits, and capital controls.

Technology

Our technology stack is designed for performance, reliability, and adaptability, enabling continuous research iteration and stable production deployment.
